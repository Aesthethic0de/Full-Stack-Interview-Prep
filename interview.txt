Topics you need to study to learn Low Level Design:

1) Basic OOPS Principles
- Encapsulation
- Abstraction
- Inheritance
- Polymorphism

2) SOLID Principles
- Single Responsibility
- Open-Closed
- Liskov Substitution
- Interface Segregation
- Dependency Inversion

3) DRY, YAGNI & KISS Principle

4) Design Patterns
- Creational: Singleton, Factory Method, Abstract Factory, Builder
- Structural: Decorator, Facade, Adapter, Composite
- Behavioral: Iterator, Observer, Command, Strategy, State

5) UML Diagrams
- Use Case Diagrams
- Activity Diagrams
- Class Diagrams
- Sequence Diagrams
